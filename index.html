Welcome Planeswalkers!

Welcome to our database of Magic: The Gathering cards. This website includes a list of all Magic cards that exist. You can access this by clicking the 'Browse' link. By clicking the 'Search' link, you can search for any Magic card that exists, this will also tell you if we have any of that specific card, and how many we have. If you are adding more cards to our collection, click the 'Insert New Card' link, and fill out the form there.

 

 

Why the Database?

Well, what exactly is a database and what does it do? Our database of Magic cards is what you would call a 'Relational Database' which, well, consists of many different relations. These relations are usually represented as tables. They contain columns of attributes and rows of values. A Database Management System is used to create and manage these databases.

Right now, you're probably thinking, "Why not just put all those cards in a list and sort through the list?" We are using a database to avoid inconsistentsies. Changing a list may cause modification problems. We can solve these problems by splitting lists into several tables.
